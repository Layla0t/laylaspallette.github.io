My Story

Hello! My name is Layla Thompson, and I’m the owner of Layla's Pallette. At Layla's Pallette, we’re all about making a bunch of different baked goods that everyone will love. We have tasty cookies, yummy brownies, light muffins, and even treats that don’t have eggs, all made with a lot of care and creativity.

I started Layla's Pallette in early April 2024 because I wanted to make sure that everyone, no matter what kind of allergies they have, they still can enjoy really good treats. Whether you’re gluten-sensitive, or vegan, I’m here to offer something that tastes great and meets your needs. I also really love coding, this website specifically, is made all by code by me. Yes! you heard that right! I coded this website all by myself, any bugs and updates will be fixed myself to make sure this website is the best as possible!!

Since opening, I’ve been working hard to get my recipes just right and to add new treats that everyone can enjoy. Each treat is made with the best ingredients and a lot of love, with the hope of making you smile with every bite.

Thanks for checking out Layla's Pallette! I hope you’ll give our baked goods a try and find something you really like.
